Mark Wahlberg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In the early 2000s, he ventured into big budget action-oriented movies, such as The Perfect Storm (2000), Planet of the Apes (2001), and The Italian Job (2003). He was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for playing a police officer in the crime drama The Departed(2006). He was nominated for the Golden Globe Award for Best Actor for the sports drama biopic The Fighter(2010), in which he starred as Micky Ward. He is also starred in Pain & Gain(2013).
